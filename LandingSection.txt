Here’s the **clean, judge-winning landing page structure** for your current product (**LegalSathi / Symentix type AI legal assistant**). This is optimized for **hackathon scoring, not decoration**.

---

# 🧠 Landing Page Sections (Final Winning Structure)

## 1. Hero Section (Hook)

**Goal:** Make judges instantly understand what it is.

* Product name: LegalSathi AI

* Tagline:

  > “Your AI legal assistant for Nepali laws, documents, and government procedures.”

* Subtext:

  > “Ask anything. Get step-by-step legal guidance instantly.”

* CTA buttons:

  * 🚀 Try Demo
  * 📄 View Features

* Optional (VERY powerful):

  * Chat input box in hero:

    > “Ask your legal question…”

---

## 2. Problem Section (Pain)

**Goal:** Make the pain feel real.

Show:

* Citizens don’t understand legal language
* Multiple office visits due to confusion
* Missing documents cause delays
* Legal help is expensive or inaccessible

One strong line:

> “Legal systems fail not because laws are complex — but because they are not explained clearly.”

---

## 3. Solution Section (What you built)

**Goal:** Introduce your product clearly.

Explain:

* AI answers legal questions
* Gives step-by-step procedures
* Lists required documents
* Explains rights in simple language
* Suggests when to consult a lawyer

Use 3–4 feature cards:

* AI Legal Chat
* Document Guidance
* Rights Explanation
* Lawyer Connection

---

## 4. How It Works (Core Logic)

**Goal:** Show intelligence without deep tech overload.

Simple flow:

```
User Question
     ↓
AI Understanding
     ↓
Legal Knowledge Retrieval
     ↓
Structured Answer
```

OR 3 steps:

1. Ask your question
2. AI analyzes legal context
3. Get structured guidance

---

## 5. Live Demo Section (MOST IMPORTANT SECTION)

**Goal:** This is where you win or lose.

Include:

* Chat UI box
* Sample prompts:

Examples:

* “How do I register a business in Nepal?”
* “What are tenant rights in Nepal?”
* “How to apply for citizenship?”

Output preview:

* Summary
* Steps
* Documents
* Warnings

---

## 6. Features Section

**Goal:** Show value clearly.

Cards:

* 🧾 Step-by-step legal guidance
* ⚖️ Rights explanation
* 📄 Document checklist
* 🚨 Mistake prevention
* 👨‍⚖️ Lawyer recommendations

---

## 7. Why It Matters (Impact Section)

**Goal:** Show real-world importance.

Split into:

### Citizens

* Save time
* Reduce confusion
* Avoid mistakes

### Government

* Fewer incorrect submissions
* Less workload

### Legal System

* Better access to justice

---

## 8. Differentiation Section (VERY IMPORTANT)

**Goal:** Prove you're not “just another chatbot”.

| Traditional Search | LegalSathi AI        |
| ------------------ | -------------------- |
| Scattered info     | Structured guidance  |
| Legal jargon       | Simple explanations  |
| No clear steps     | Step-by-step answers |
| Passive search     | Active assistant     |

---

## 9. Future Vision Section

**Goal:** Show scalability.

* Nepali language support
* Document scanning AI
* Lawyer marketplace
* Voice assistant
* Mobile app

---

## 10. Footer (Minimal)

* Project name
* Team name (Symentix or LegalSathi)
* GitHub link
* Hackathon note

---

# ⚠️ What NOT to include

❌ Login/signup
❌ Pricing
❌ Blog/news section
❌ Complex animations
❌ Too many sections (>10 is bad)
❌ Technical architecture on landing page
